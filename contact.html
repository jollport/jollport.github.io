Frequently Asked Questions

What are your service hours?

Our standard business hours are Monday-Friday 8AM-8PM and Saturday-Sunday 9AM-5PM. However, we offer 24/7 emergency services for urgent electrical and critical technical issues.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes! We offer free phone estimates for most services. For complex projects, we provide free on-site consultations to assess your needs and provide an accurate quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, check, credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), and digital payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay). For larger projects, we offer payment plans.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely! We are fully licensed electricians (#EL123456) and carry $2 million in liability insurance. We're also certified for computer repair and network installation.

How quickly can you respond to emergencies?

We guarantee a 1-2 hour response time for emergency calls within our primary service area. For life-threatening electrical emergencies, we'll dispatch immediately.
