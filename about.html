Meet Patrick - Your Multi-Skill Technician

Hello! I'm Patrick, the founder and lead technician at Patrick Technician & Electrician. With over 15 years of experience in the technical services industry, I've built a reputation for reliable, high-quality work across multiple disciplines.

My journey began as an electrical apprentice, where I developed a passion for solving complex technical problems. Over the years, I expanded my skills to include computer repair, network setup, and home entertainment systems because I noticed my clients needed a single, trustworthy technician who could handle all their technical needs.
